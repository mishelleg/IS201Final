Music

Sports

  • Swimming: I've done swim lessons since I was a baby and began swimming competitively on a club team around third grade. I also swam on my high school swim team.
  • Basketball: I only played basketball for one year in elementary school, but I still really enjoy playing and watching the sport.
  • Volleyball: Volleyball is just a really sport that I love. I'm really bad, but I still have fun playing with my friends and family.

Traveling
